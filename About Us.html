Welcome to Holy Yadha Course (HYC)...

The HYC was established in 2005 and is located in Kelurahan Tugu Selatan, Kecamatan Koja, North Jakarta. We provide a tutoring program specifically for English and Mathematics subjects for elementary school students. Since 2005, HYC has been shown successful in enhancing the learning abilities of students from various top-notch primary schools, both national and international. The philosophy and teaching methods implemented in the HYC course focus on developing commendable character, competent hard skills, and proficient soft skills. The instructor is a graduate of a state university with a Bachelor's degree in teacher education, and has over 30 years of experience as a guidance head teacher.
